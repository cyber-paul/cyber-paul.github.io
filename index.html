Consultant-Centered Structure

TransitSpace Systems LLC operates with a consultant-first philosophy. Our LLC is designed to serve not only as a delivery agency, but also as a legal and operational platform for independent cybersecurity consultants and early-career professionals seeking to enter federal, state, or regional markets.

  • Maximum margin retention: All consultants retain the majority share of contract payouts.
  • Transparent cost model: Overhead and processing fees are fixed, disclosed, and never arbitrary.
  • No benefits, no dilution: This LLC does not offer traditional employment benefits, but instead delivers high-margin consultant payouts aligned to service value.
  • DBA support welcomed: Individuals operating under brands (like CyberPaul.space) can work through the LLC while maintaining their unique identity.

Our model offers a flexible path for consultants to win competitive bids through a shared infrastructure, while building community resilience and maintaining full independence.
